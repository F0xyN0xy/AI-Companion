Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AI Companion ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the AI Companion desktop application ("the App"). We are committed to being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Your first name and email address
  • A bcrypt-hashed password — your actual password is never stored or readable
  • Your email verification status and account creation date

We do not collect payment information, location data, device identifiers, or any usage analytics.

2. How We Store Your Data

Account data (name, email, hashed password, verification status) is stored in JSONBin, a third-party JSON storage service. You can review JSONBin's privacy practices at jsonbin.io/privacy-policy.

All of your chat history, companion settings, and preferences are stored locally on your own device in ~/.ai-companion/. This data never leaves your machine and we have no access to it.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and authenticate your account
  • To send you a one-time email verification link when you register
  • To identify your local data folder so your settings persist across sessions

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with several third-party services. When you use the App, your messages and prompts may be processed by:

  • Groq — AI inference provider. See groq.com/privacy-policy
  • OpenRouter — AI model routing. See openrouter.ai/privacy
  • Discord — Rich Presence status (optional, only if Discord is running). See discord.com/privacy

We use Gmail SMTP solely to deliver your verification email. No email content or metadata is retained by us.

5. Email Communications

We will only ever send you one email: a verification link when you create your account. We do not send newsletters, promotional emails, or any other communications.

6. Data Retention

Your account data remains in our database for as long as your account exists. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Local data stored on your device can be deleted by removing the ~/.ai-companion/ folder.

7. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us at: privacy@aicompanion.app
