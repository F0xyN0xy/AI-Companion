What makes it different

Built to feel real

Most AI companions feel static after a few conversations. This one doesn't.

🧠
Evolving Memory
Remembers facts, preferences, and important events across every conversation. Core memories never fade. Others shift in importance over time.
💬
Personality Drift
Five personality traits — warmth, confidence, playfulness, openness, assertiveness — that genuinely shift based on your conversations over weeks.
🌊
Deep Talk Mode
Automatically detects emotional or philosophical topics and switches to a deeper reasoning model. Toggle it manually anytime.
🎭
Fully Customisable
Name, personality, avatar, colour — make them entirely yours. The companion adapts to whoever you build.
🧱
Real Autonomy
Your companion can disagree, change the subject, express discomfort, or push back. Not a servant — a person.
🎮
Discord Rich Presence
Shows what you're up to in Discord — companion name, current mood, time spent chatting. Fully integrated.
